Who are we ?

Why ?

Loneliness is a real problem these days. Many people suffer from this isolation on a daily basis which can be caused by multiple reasons. Indeed some people simply have trouble creating links, talking and opening up to others. It is for these people that we decided to create our solution. A solution that consists in bringing together, creating contact and sharing good times around an activity that you cannot do alone. Bowling, the cinema or even hiking are good reasons to get together so take the first step! Create your events and share unforgettable moments with your future friends.

" We must come together, unite our energies, to create together a world based on solidarity. " - Serge Desjardins
